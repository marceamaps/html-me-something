Here are a list of my favorite places to visit in Italy. I lived in Italy in 2015 and got travel around the entire country. Now I'd like to share with you some of my most favorite destinations and where I recommend people visit!

A Word on Italy

While Italy is regarded as one of the most beautiful places on earth, it's important to remember that the people who live there want to keep it this way! Remember when traveling to be respectful of Italians and their customs, and that their lifestyle may be more different from yours than you think.

Logistics

Italy is on the €, but cards are widely accepted. It's always a good idea to notify your bank before you go. Travel is usually done through public transit, so brush up on your italian before you go!
